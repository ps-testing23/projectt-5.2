About Us

We are an organization dedicated to reviving the ancient science of traditional home remedies to enhance well-being, particularly focusing on women's health. In the realm of traditional healing, laddoos hold a significant place. They are not just sweet treats but also carriers of nourishment and healing. Embracing the wisdom of our ancestors, we offer you laddoos infused with the essence of time-honored remedies, crafted to rejuvenate and restore vitality. Our startup is specifically tailored towards addressing women's health concerns, including addressing iron deficiency, with utmost care towards women's well-being
